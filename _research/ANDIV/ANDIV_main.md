The ANDIV project is a collaboration between the universities of Munich, WÃ¼rzburg and Jena. The goal is to investigate tropical insect microbiota along an elevational gradient in the <strong>Peruvian Andes</strong>. This includes the four major holometabolous insect groups Hymenoptera, Coleoptera, Diptera and Lepidoptera. Among the Lepidoptera order, I focus on the families of <strong>Pieridae</strong> and <strong>Heliconidae</strong> to disentangle environmental drivers of microbiome assembly from phylogenetically conserved core-microbiota pattern. This combines the use of Metabarcoding for butterfly gut-microbiota analysis and Barcoding for insect identification.
